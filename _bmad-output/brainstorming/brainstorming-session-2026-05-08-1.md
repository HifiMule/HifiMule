## Session Overview

**Topic:** Finding a new product name for HifiMule
**Goals:** A memorable, server-agnostic name that evokes music + sync + portability, does not tie to any specific server or device brand, and has strong branding potential

### Session Setup

HifiMule is a Tauri-based desktop app that syncs music from self-hosted media servers (Jellyfin, expanding to Navidrome and Subsonic) to portable devices (USB mass storage, MTP devices, iPod). The current name is too server-specific and won't scale to the broader multi-server, multi-device vision. A new name is needed.

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques
**Analysis Context:** Product naming for a multi-server music sync tool with focus on memorability, brand independence, and evoking music + portability

**Recommended Techniques:**

- **First Principles Thinking:** Strips to core product identity before naming begins — prevents anchoring on "sync" or "music" prematurely
- **Analogical Thinking:** Draws parallels from music history, travel, and technology to surface rich metaphors that become name candidates
- **Concept Blending:** Merges the strongest concepts found in phases 1 and 2 into genuinely novel name combinations

**AI Rationale:** Product naming benefits most from starting with identity clarity (what IS this, at its core?) before moving to metaphor mining (what does it feel like?) and finally creative synthesis (what new word/concept captures it?). This sequence prevents the common trap of generating names that describe features rather than express character.
